This is a 2D dungeon game where you fight monsters with a sword and loot chests
while moving around. You are also able to upgrade your weapon via the menu button provided and can also select a
character of your choice. Animations are included, and to make the gameplay more interesting, you will get pushed
back when an enemy attacks you. The attack damage will be shown, and you can also heal your health back using the
healing fountains placed within the maps.
